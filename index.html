Why Correlation-One?:

I chose to partake in Correlation One's Software Development course for the following reasons:

  • To have better career opportunities
  • To help me be financially free
  • Help me get back into programming
  • To help me create a better future for me and those around me

I made the commitment to not only relearn old web development skills through Correlation-One but, to learn new ones as well. My inspiration to take the software development course came from the want to get a career opportunity outside of Amazon. Don't get the wrong impression, I love being in IT with Amazon and the company has taught me many skills and the opportunities that the company has given me(like being able to take this course), but I am at the point where I am basically burnt out every day I go to work. When I seen this show up in Career Choice, I decided to jump on it immediately and give Correlation-One a chance. I have really enjoyed the courses and I am already slowly getting back into programming again. Some of the things that I learned that was cool to learn was the favicons, emojis, and forms. Hopefully as this course progresses, I can learn javascript, Bootstrap or Angular, working on projects as a team, and designing web pages.
