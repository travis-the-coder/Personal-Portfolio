Hey There!

Who am I:

My name is Travis Ojeda. I was born in Syracuse, New York on June 18, 1993. I love video games and the consoles that they are played on since I was a kid. I love to find ways to get things to work and how to make them better than they were when they first came out. As someone who always wanted to be hands on and be creative, I have found ways to use such skills. As an example of this, I picked up soldering and plastic welding in hopes to repair and modify various things.
I am an easy going person who wants to have the best life that one could have. I do like to see and understand how certain processes work.

As I got older, I developed a new like/upcoming passion in the works: programming and repairing. When I was in college in 2013, I had taken some courses in HTML, CSS, C Programming, and Java and I really liked it. Fast forward to 2023, I repair laptops, iPhones, Android phones, tablets, and desktops. I have not yet dabbled in repairing game consoles but I plans on trying that one day.

Why Correlation-One?:

I chose to partake in Correlation One's Software Development course for the following reasons:

  • To have better career opportunities

  • To help me create a better future for me and those around me

I made the commitment to not only relearn old web development skills through Correlation-One but, to learn new ones as well. My inspiration to take the software development course came from the want to get a career opportunity outside of Amazon. Hopefully as this course progresses, I can learn javascript, Bootstrap or Angular, working on projects as a team, and designing web pages.
